Beware of account takeovers

▼

Scammers may manipulate victims to change their Singpass contact details, e.g. email and mobile number. This allows the scammers to receive the victim's One-Time Passwords or take over the Singpass account to commit crimes. If you suspect your account has been compromised, call the Singpass hotline.
